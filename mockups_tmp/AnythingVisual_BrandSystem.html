02 — Typography
The Type System
Two geometric sans-serif typefaces. Chakra Petch for display, identity, and UI structure — precise, architectural, cinematic. DM Sans for body copy and reading — humanist proportions with geometric roots, warm and legible at small sizes.
Chakra Petch — Display & Identity
Wordmark · Headlines · Labels · Navigation · Badges
Aa Bb Cc
Dd Ee Ff Gg Hh Ii Jj Kk Ll Mm
Nn Oo Pp Qq Rr Ss Tt Uu Vv Ww Xx Yy Zz · 0123456789
ANYTHINGVISUAL · SCRIPT INTELLIGENCE ENGINE · SCENE BOARD
DM Sans — Body & Reading
Body copy · UI text · Input fields · Captions · Descriptions
Aa Bb Cc
The intelligence layer between idea and visual execution. Transform raw concepts into structured visual systems that power filmmaking, marketing, and digital storytelling.
Drop your rough idea, bullet points, or partial script. The Script Intelligence Engine parses your input, detects scene boundaries, extracts characters, assigns emotional tone, and surfaces visual intelligence for every scene — ready for image generation.
