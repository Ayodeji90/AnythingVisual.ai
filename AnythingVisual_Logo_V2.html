The Mark
Four curved iris blades open around a lens eye — abstracting the camera iris into pure geometric form. The specular highlight and layered depth make it feel alive. At small sizes it reads as a pupil. At large sizes, a cinematic instrument.
The Wordmark
Chakra Petch — a geometric, slightly technical sans-serif with cinematic precision. "Anything" in white, "Visual" in muted grey: a deliberate split that mirrors the product's duality — any input, structured visual output.
Why it works
The iris is simultaneously an eye (seeing clearly), a lens (cinematic optics), and a geometric abstraction. It answers both briefs. It scales from favicon to billboard. No other AI platform has claimed this mark.

The Mark
Five vertical bars of varying widths and luminosities — a slit-scan exposure frozen in time. The bright central bar is the focal plane: where raw signal becomes clear image. Entirely geometric, zero literal meaning, unmistakably cinematic.
The Wordmark
Playfair Display Bold — commanding editorial serif. The weight and drama of Playfair anchors the otherwise abstract icon. The combination feels like a film title card. Sophisticated but not precious.
Why it works
The slit metaphor is the product: a scanner that reads the raw and outputs clarity. The graduated bars visualise the transformation from noise to precision — exactly what the Script Intelligence Engine does. Deeply ownable.

The Mark
Four arcs converging onto a single reticle + bright central dot. The arcs are camera-lens fieldlines; the reticle is precision optics; the dot is the focal point — where everything becomes clear. Meditative, precise, cinematic.
The Wordmark
DM Serif Display — editorial, warm, literary. "Anything" in roman, "Visual" in italic — a typographic duality that mirrors the product: structured intelligence (roman) meeting expressive output (italic). Quiet confidence.
Why it works
The focal point is the exact metaphor for the product: converging chaos (raw ideas, fragments, scripts) into a single point of structured visual clarity. It reads as both a lens and a target. Luxury feel. Highest versatility across contexts.
